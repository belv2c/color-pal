What are HTML color codes?

Color codes represent the colors red, green and blue in a format that computers are able to interpret and display. Commonly used formats are Hex color codes, RGB, HSL, and HTML color names.

Hex Color Codes

A hexidecimal is a six digit numeric representation of a color which is split into three parts. Hex values are composed of numbers from 0 to 9 and letters from A to F.
